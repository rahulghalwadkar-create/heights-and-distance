üéØ What are Heights and Distances?

Heights and Distances is the practical application of trigonometry to find heights of objects (like buildings, towers, trees) or distances between objects when direct measurement isn't possible. We use angles and trigonometric ratios to solve these problems!

Why is this important?

  • Used by surveyors to measure land and buildings
  • Helps pilots calculate flight paths and landing angles
  • Essential for architects and construction engineers
  • Navigation and GPS technology rely on these principles
